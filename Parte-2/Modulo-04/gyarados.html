Gyarados

About Gyarados

Gyarados is a Water/Flying type Pokémon that evolves from the humble Magikarp. Once evolved, it becomes a force of nature with access to moves like Hydro Pump, Ice Beam, and Dragon Dance.

Why He's on My Team

With high attack and an intimidating presence, Gyarados is my go-to against Fire, Ground, and Rock types. His versatility is unmatched.
