Charizard

About Charizard

Charizard is a Fire/Flying-type Pokémon that evolves from Charmeleon. With its powerful wings and flaming tail, it’s known for devastating attacks like Flamethrower, Fire Blast, and Fly.

Why He's on My Team

Charizard is my lead attacker when facing Grass, Bug, Ice, or Steel types. His speed and power make him a strong finisher, and he’s always the coolest Pokémon in battle.
