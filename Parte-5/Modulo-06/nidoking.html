Nidoking

About Nidoking

Nidoking is a dual-type Poison/Ground Pokémon known for its strength, versatility, and intimidating horn. With access to a wide variety of moves like Earthquake, Thunderbolt, Ice Beam, and Megahorn, Nidoking is a strategic powerhouse in battle.

Why He's on My Team

Nidoking’s ability to counter many types makes him invaluable against the Elite Four. His strength lies not only in his stats, but also in the unpredictability of his moveset.
