Jolteon

About Jolteon

Jolteon is an Electric-type Pokémon, known for its incredible speed and special attack. It can use moves like Thunderbolt, Shadow Ball, and Double Kick to keep enemies off guard.

Why He's on My Team

Jolteon is the perfect counter to Water and Flying types. With amazing speed and powerful electric attacks, he often strikes first — and last.
