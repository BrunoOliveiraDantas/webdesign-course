Dragonite

About Dragonite

Dragonite is a Dragon/Flying-type Pokémon, often underestimated for its gentle look. However, it has incredible attack and defense stats, and can learn moves like Hyper Beam, Dragon Claw, and Outrage.

Why He's on My Team

As my closer, Dragonite offers balance, power, and resilience. He’s a great option when I need bulk, coverage, and raw strength — plus, he flies!
