Primeape

About Primeape

Primeape is a Fighting-type Pokémon known for its speed and fury. Always in a rage, it never backs down from a challenge. With powerful moves like Cross Chop, Seismic Toss, and Rock Slide, it's a tough opponent.

Why He's on My Team

Primeape is fast, aggressive, and reliable when I need to take down Ice or Rock types. His no-nonsense attitude adds fire to my team.
